The Open Treaty

A living document, shaped by humanity. What must the world agree upon before artificial intelligence reshapes it? Your voice belongs here.

Open to all nations, all disciplines, all people Initiated 2026
We, the participants of this treaty, believe that the development of artificial intelligence is among the most consequential decisions in human history. This form exists not to govern, but to listen — to gather the unfiltered wisdom, fears, hopes, and demands of people everywhere. Every response contributes to a living document that will be published openly for all to see.
