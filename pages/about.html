About This Project

Why This Matters

The Open Treaty is not a legal document. It is not a petition. It is not a manifesto. It is an open microphone for humanity.
Purpose

What We're Building

Artificial intelligence is being developed at unprecedented speed, largely behind closed doors. The decisions being made today — by a small number of labs, companies, and governments — will reshape every aspect of human life.

Yet most people have never been asked what they think should happen. No referendum. No consultation. No voice.

The Open Treaty exists to change that.

Principles

How This Works

No Gatekeeping
Anyone, anywhere, can contribute.

No credentials required. No registration. No barriers. Your perspective matters whether you're a researcher, a student, a parent, or someone who's never used AI before.

Total Transparency
Every response is published unedited.

Nothing is filtered. Nothing is cherry-picked. If you submit it, the world sees it — exactly as you wrote it.

Living Document
This treaty evolves as more voices join.

Responses are aggregated and published openly. Patterns emerge. Consensus forms. Disagreements remain visible. This is not about finding one answer — it's about mapping the full spectrum of human opinion.

Origins

Who Built This

The Open Treaty was initiated by The AI Lyceum, an independent research collective focused on AI governance, ethics, and public understanding.

We believe that the most important conversations about AI are happening without the people who will be most affected by it. This project is our attempt to fix that.

The source code for this project is open. The data is open. The conversation is open.
